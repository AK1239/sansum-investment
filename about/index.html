Our Story

Building a modern beverage manufacturing facility in Tanzania with innovation, quality, and dedication to consumer satisfaction.

Who We Are

Established in May 2023, Sansum Investment Co. Limited is a wholly-owned Chinese enterprise situated in Kibaha, Coast Region, Tanzania. Our state-of-the-art facility spans 106 mu (approximately 17.6 acres) and represents an investment of 35 million RMB.

Our modern, garden-style factory integrates office space, production facilities, warehousing, and accommodation, creating an efficient and comfortable working environment.
